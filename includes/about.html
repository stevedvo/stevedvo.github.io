About Me

Spreadsheet, data and programming enthusiast.

Having spent my teens in the 90's I have grown up with computers and followed with interest the advances in technology and the emergence of the Internet. Throughout my education and employment I have used a variety of platforms: from BBC Micros, Acorns & Atari STs through to various versions of Apple & Microsoft operating systems & environments.

Studying Mathematics, Physics, Computing, and Astronomy at college and university gave me some excellent skills with regards to problem-solving, logical thinking, programming and the ability to accurately capture data and process it in an efficient and useful manner.

Working for MoD contractors for 9+ years I have honed my skills in data-analysis: my main duties involving the exporting & merging job & accounts data from a variety of bespoke software applications into Excel and producing a number of daily/weekly/monthly/ad hoc reports to monitor operational & commercial performance. I always relished the opportunity to try something new, to think about how to break down the data in different ways to measure performance and how to utilise my generated reports to optimise business processes and maximise margins.

I am self-studying to further my knowledge of HTML5, PHP, MySQL, JavaScript and CSS3 to open up new opportunities & challenges in web development and data & project management, and have put my skills to good use by joining DVO Media as Product Owner & Client Services Manager.
