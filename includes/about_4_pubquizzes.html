I like all sorts of quizzes and panel games at home and on the telly: Trivial Pursuit, QI, Have I Got News For You, University Challenge - and I do rather enjoy taking part in pub quizzes when the opportunity arises.

Unfortunately I - and the small team of 2/3 that I might be part of - very rarely do well enough to beat the competition of older/wiser/larger teams, but it is always good fun to sink a few pints of ale and have a go.

There are a couple of local pubs that I have found put on a quiz every week or two, and have a fine selection of real ales to boot:

  • The White Hart - quiz every Tuesday evening with food served halfway through
  • Waggon And Horses - quiz on alternate Thursdays
