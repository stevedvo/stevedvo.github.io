Stand Up Comedy is one of my favourite forms of entertainment. In my youth I remember being in stitches watching on the telly performers such as Jack Dee, Jasper Carrot, Phil Cool, Ben Elton, and numerous other comics that populated primetime tv slots on a Saturday evening.

As I got into my mid-teens and older, I watched and bought more videos of recorded stand-up shows - Lee Evans was always a particular favourite and would crack me up without fail. Some comedians I've known of due to them already being well-established, others I've come across as they were just starting out in their careers and did short guest spots on various other stand-up / panel shows.
